--- FORM INTERACTION ---
Normalise quotes before grepping (HTML may use single or double quotes):
    NORM=$(echo "$PAGE" | tr "'" '"')

    USER_FIELD=$(echo "$NORM" | grep -o '<input[^>]*>' | grep -v 'type="password"' | grep -v 'type="hidden"' | grep -o 'name="[^"]*"' | head -1 | cut -d'"' -f2 || true)

Extract CSRF / hidden token:

    EXTRA=$([ -n "$TOKEN_NAME" ] && echo "&${TOKEN_NAME}=${TOKEN_VAL}" || true)

--- SQL INJECTION ---
Target a specific known endpoint and parameter — do NOT use --forms (too slow, scans the whole app):
